The Property

A Dogtrot Design Built to Grow

The facility is modeled on the classic East Texas dogtrot style — a design that is both deeply rooted in Southern tradition and purpose-built for expandability.

🌳 One Acre 🏡 Dogtrot Architecture 📐 Expandable Layout 🐕 Outdoor Training Areas 🅿 Ample Parking 💡 Well-Lit Interior Spaces 🪨 Stone & Wood Construction 🌿 Landscaped Grounds

About the Dogtrot Style: The traditional dogtrot cabin — two rooms connected by an open breezeway — is an iconic piece of East Texas heritage. This design not only provides natural ventilation and an inviting covered outdoor space, but also allows the facility to be expanded over time as programs and community needs grow.
